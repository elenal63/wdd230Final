The Beautiful Southern California Coast

Location

  • located along the San Diego County coastline, about 30 miles north of downtown San Diego
  • stretches along 7 miles of shoreline, from Oceanside to the north and Encinitas to the south

Beaches

  • some popular beaches are South Carlsbad State Beach, Carlsbad Village Beach, and northern sections of Carlsbad State Beach
  • best known for swimming, surfing, beach-going, and coastal views

Terrain & Geography

  • coastline faces due west leading to ideal sunsets over the ocean
  • coastal headlands separate small pocket beaches creating a mix of long open beaches and sheltered coves
  • lagoons and marshes lie behind some beaches, like Batiquitos Lagoon behind Carlsbad State Beach

Recreation & Attractions

  • strong tourism sector and beach culture, with Ocean Street being a hub
  • Variety of beach resorts, hotels, restaurants, shops and attractions near downtown and along the coast
  • other major attractions include Legoland California Resort and The Flower Fields
